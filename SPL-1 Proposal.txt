Title: GES Calculator

This calculator provides following functionalities:

•Graph of constant functions, linear function, quadratic function, polynomial function exponential function,
 trigonometric function, logarithmic function, ellipse, parabola, hyperbola etc.
•Solve linear equation and polynomial equation of n th order.
•Numerical expression (infix, postfix, prefix) conversion.
•Numbering conversion in different bases.
•Some functionality of a simple scientific calculator and programmer calculator.

Programming Language: Java/JavaFX.
